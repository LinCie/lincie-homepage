# Design Context

## Users
General public visitors viewing a personal website/blog. The site should feel welcoming and accessible to anyone, with intuitive navigation and readable content.

## Brand Personality
- **Minimal**: Clean layouts, generous whitespace, no visual clutter
- **Cute**: Soft, friendly aesthetic with subtle charm—think rounded corners, gentle details, approachable feel
- **Calm**: Peaceful, uncluttered experience that doesn't overwhelm—serene and relaxing to use
- **Elegant**: Refined and sophisticated without being pretentious—subtle beauty in the details

## Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Light mode with soft pastel palette
- **Colors**: Pastel palette including soft pinks, lavenders, mint greens, and soft peaches
- **Visual tone**: Gentle, inviting, and harmonious—avoiding harsh contrasts or aggressive elements
- **References**: Clean personal sites with character; nothing corporate or generic

## Design Principles
1. **Embrace whitespace**: Generous margins and padding create breathing room and calm
2. **Soft edges**: Use rounded corners (border-radius) to convey friendliness and warmth
3. **Pastel palette**: Apply muted, soft colors as accents against clean backgrounds—avoid saturated or harsh colors
4. **Typography as design**: Choose a distinctive, readable typeface; let type carry visual interest rather than heavy decoration
5. **Subtle details matter**: Small intentional touches (hover states, transitions, micro-interactions) add charm without clutter

## Technical Notes
- Framework: Astro (static site generator)
- Accessibility target: WCAG AA standard
- No dark mode required at this stage
